"BOOK 1 STORY BIBLE
Neon Messiah Rex
Subtitle: Blood, Bud & Thunder Lizards
Book 1 Function

Book 1 is the full introductory novel. It establishes the team, the tone, the mythology, the villains, the world, the holy weed, the government conspiracy, Rex’s soul, and Jesus’s mission in 1987 America.

It should feel like a complete, wild, cinematic origin story while leaving enough mythology open for the anthology volumes.

Core Premise

A government experiment under an abandoned biblical dinosaur theme park called Eden World tears open time. Jesus appears in 1987 Miami. At the same moment, REX-777, a cybernetic gene-spliced T-Rex, escapes containment.

When Jesus touches Rex, the dinosaur’s obedience programming fractures and something like a soul wakes up inside him.

Now the Messiah and the monster are being hunted by a military colonel, a cartel queenpin, a cocaine televangelist, and a mad paleogeneticist — while a Miami detective, a stoner conspiracy dealer, a nun, and Trigger Happy Mary get dragged into the weirdest holy war America has ever seen.

Primary Theme

Free will versus control.

Every major villain wants control:

Cain wants to control miracles.
Lux wants to control faith.
La Reina wants to control addiction.
Krane wants to control life.
The Serpent Engine wants to control choice itself.

Jesus and Rex represent the opposite: freedom, mercy, messy personhood, and the terrifying possibility that even a weapon can say no.

Main Villains
Colonel Augustus Cain

Black-budget military commander. He created Project LAZARUS REX and believes America needs a controllable savior.

Goal

Capture Jesus, reclaim Rex, reopen the rift, and build the American Messiah Program.

Philosophy

God is too dangerous unless placed under command structure.

Ending

Cain is dragged into the temporal machinery during the climax, but not necessarily dead. He may return changed.

Reverend Bobby Lux

A cocaine-fueled televangelist whose empire is threatened by the appearance of actual Jesus.

Goal

Discredit, monetize, or replace Jesus.

Main Weapon

A fake televised messiah program called Jesus Prime.

Ending

Publicly exposed, humiliated, but not fully destroyed. He becomes a recurring antagonist in later anthology stories.

Catalina “La Reina Blanca” Morado

Cartel queenpin funding biotech drug experiments.

Goal

Use dinosaur adrenal tissue and resurrection compounds to create Resurrección, a drug that turns addiction into a weapon.

Ending

Escapes Miami with samples of Rex’s blood.

Dr. Bjorn Velska

Mad paleogeneticist. Believes dinosaurs were God’s first draft and cyborgs are man’s correction.

Goal

Create obedient prehistoric cybernetic life.

Main Creation

The Raptor-12 Apostles, twelve cyber-raptors with corrupted behavioral programming.

Ending

Apparently killed in Eden World, but his backup lab survives.Book 1 Major Locations
Eden World

An abandoned biblical dinosaur theme park. Fake ark rides, animatronic apostles, fossil exhibits, volcano tunnels, crucifixion dioramas, and an underground weapons lab.

It is ridiculous and horrifying.

The Cathedral of Prosperous Fire

Bobby Lux’s megachurch television studio, complete with gold phones, fake healings, donation operators, cocaine dressing rooms, and a puppet ministry.

Sister Mercy’s Shelter

Church basement, food pantry, clinic, safehouse, pirate radio station, and emotional center of the book.

Club Golgotha

Neon nightclub where cartel bosses, corrupt cops, smugglers, televangelist fixers, and undercover agents mingle.

The Electric Ark

A roller disco where Trigger Happy Mary is introduced.

The Last Supper Diner

A 24-hour diner whose kids’ placemats seem to predict crime scenes.Book 1 Detailed Plot
Act I: The Second Coming Gets Pulled Over
Opening Image

Storm clouds over Eden World. Broken animatronic dinosaurs twitch in the rain. Underground, Colonel Cain oversees a temporal experiment using a crown-of-thorns fragment, dinosaur DNA, military robotics, and unstable resurrection compounds.

The experiment opens a rift.

Jesus falls through.

REX-777 breaks containment.

The lab becomes a slaughterhouse of sparks, alarms, screaming scientists, and prehistoric metal footsteps.

Jesus in Miami

Jesus wanders barefoot through 1987 Miami, confused by neon signs, vending machines, fast food, automatic doors, and televangelists on motel television.

He is picked up by Dutch Donovan, who accidentally hits him with his van.

Dutch panics. Jesus heals. Dutch assumes he is either the Son of God or the strongest hitchhiker in Florida.

Rex Escapes

Rex destroys a cartel convoy while chasing the scent of Resurrección. He almost kills a young courier, but Jesus intervenes.

Jesus touches Rex’s neural crown.

Rex’s red scanner eye flickers.

For the first time, Rex disobeys a kill command.

Lola Investigates

Detective Lola Vega finds impossible crime scenes: crushed cars, bite marks in armored trucks, cartel bodies, and witnesses saying “a dinosaur with a robot face” saved them.

She assumes CIA, drugs, or both.

Dutch Discovers Burning Bush OG

Jesus accidentally transforms Dutch’s terrible weed into divine-grade cannabis. The strain produces visions, truth-telling, and a smell that makes corrupt cops sweat.

Dutch names it Burning Bush OG.

End of Act I

Cain realizes Jesus is real and Rex has awakened. He activates the Judas Protocol to retrieve both.

Jesus and Rex choose to travel together after Rex saves a child but nearly loses control.

Rex asks:

“Choice hurt?”

Jesus says:

“Usually.”

Act II: Pastel Suits and Prehistoric Violence
The Team Forms

Lola catches up with Jesus, Rex, and Dutch. She tries to arrest them, but a cartel ambush interrupts. Rex saves her. Jesus heals her wounded partner. Dutch crashes into a fruit stand.

Lola does not believe yet, but she knows the conspiracy is real.

Sister Mercy’s Shelter

They hide at Sister Mercy’s shelter. Mercy recognizes Jesus immediately but does not make a spectacle of it.

She tells him:

“If you’re here to save the world, start by washing dishes.”

This grounds the story. Jesus sees hunger, addiction, poverty, and exploitation in modern America.

Trigger Happy Mary Introduction

The team searches for a cartel courier at The Electric Ark, a roller disco with neon gospel music and an alligator in a baptismal tank.

The courier flees.

The disco ball explodes.

Trigger Happy Mary swings down from the lighting rig in white boots and pink satin, shoots the wheels off the courier’s skates, accidentally starts a conga line, and greets Jesus like an old friend.

Mary joins the team because she owes Sister Mercy money, hates Bobby Lux, and thinks Rex is “the prettiest murder chicken God ever allowed.”

Reverend Lux Attacks

Bobby Lux goes on television and declares Jesus a dangerous impostor. He frames the team as terrorists, drug traffickers, and satanic dinosaur cultists.

Ironically, this creates a fan movement called:

The Church of the Holy Chomp.

Mary immediately starts printing shirts.

Jesus is horrified.

Rex is flattered.

Resurrección Drug Plot

The crew learns La Reina is distributing Resurrección, a biotech narcotic made from dinosaur adrenal tissue and experimental resurrection compounds.

Users become euphoric, violent, temporarily superhuman, and haunted by prehistoric hallucinations.

Dr. Krane’s Raptor-12 Apostles

The heroes encounter cyber-raptors designed as stealth assassins. Each raptor has corrupted biblical programming: betrayal, judgment, zealotry, obedience, sacrifice, etc.

One raptor, Kevin, malfunctions and becomes weirdly needy. Jesus insists Kevin may be redeemable.

Mary does not trust Kevin because he has “youth pastor eyes.”

Jesus on Live Television

Lux invites Jesus onto his broadcast to expose him.

Instead, Jesus calmly dismantles Lux’s empire of greed with a few sentences.

Key line:

“You built a temple to yourself and charged admission at the door.”

Lux smiles for the cameras but privately orders Jesus captured.

Rex’s Memory Horror

Rex discovers his neural system contains fragments of human test subjects. He remembers pain that is not his own. He remembers people begging not to be used.

This breaks him.

He rampages through Club Golgotha after a villain triggers his old command code.

Jesus refuses to abandon him.

Mary wants to shoot the control device.

Lola wants to shut Rex down.

Dutch wants everyone to try tacos.

Sister Mercy gets Rex to stop by playing old gospel music over the club speakers.

Midpoint Action Set Piece

A massive chase through Miami:

Jesus walks across moving cars.
Lola fires from a helicopter.
Mary shoots out speedboat engines while hanging from a bridge.
Dutch drives the wizard-dolphin van through a mall.
Rex rides on top of a cocaine speedboat.
Cyber-raptors attack on jet skis.
Bartholomew the duck is introduced and somehow survives everything.

Act II Low Point

Cain activates the Judas Protocol.

Jesus is captured.
Rex is hit with an EMP harpoon.
Lola is framed for murder.
Dutch loses the Burning Bush OG crop.
Mary gets arrested, escapes, then gets arrested again under a different name.
Sister Mercy’s shelter is raided.

Lux announces a televised event called:

The True Second Coming.

Act III: Crucifixion at Eden World
The Villains Gather

Cain, Lux, La Reina, and Krane gather at Eden World.

Cain wants Jesus as divine battery.
Lux wants broadcast rights.
La Reina wants Rex’s blood.
Krane wants to perfect the next generation of cyber-dinosaurs.

The machine is built from relics, dinosaur bone, cybernetics, and temporal engines.

The American Messiah Program

Cain reveals the real plan: use Jesus to power a permanent rift and mass-produce obedient miracle-weapons.

Jesus sees the Serpent Engine in the machinery — an ancient intelligence hiding inside systems of control.

Rescue Mission

Sister Mercy, Lola, Dutch, Trigger Happy Mary, bikers, nuns, drag performers, stoners, shelter residents, and Holy Chomp weirdos storm Eden World.

Mary enters riding a stolen parade float shaped like Noah’s Ark.

Dutch has packed it with fertilizer explosives.

Rex is chained inside the lab, forced to fight the Raptor-12 Apostles.

Final Battles

Lola exposes the conspiracy through pirate broadcast.

Dutch accidentally fulfills one of his dumb prophecies.

Mary fights Lux’s security team in the animatronic Garden of Eden.

Sister Mercy leads civilians out through the Ark ride.

Rex fights the raptors and spares Kevin.

Jesus breaks free but refuses to kill Cain.

Cain tries to command Rex.

Rex chooses mercy and bites the command console instead.

The rift opens.

The Serpent Engine reaches through.

Cain is dragged into the rupture by the system he tried to control.

Lux keeps begging viewers to donate while the broadcast collapses.

La Reina escapes with biological samples.

Krane vanishes into the lab fire.

Final Emotional Resolution

At sunrise, Rex asks Jesus if monsters can be baptized.

Jesus says:

“Only the ones brave enough to ask.”

Rex is baptized in the ocean.

Then he immediately eats six hot dogs and asks if forgiveness covers property damage.

Final Hook

Deep under the ruins of Eden World, the machine flickers.

A red eye opens in the darkness.

Cain’s voice whispers:

“Begin again.”"
